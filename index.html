About Us:

The University of Oregon Drawing & Graphic Arts Club exists for artists and art lovers of all skill levels and disciplines to find community, work on and share their art, and discuss works created by others in a casual and non-academic setting. We meet twice a week, and there is no formal application process or rigid hierarchy within the club.

  • Founded: April 2023
  • Members: 120+
  • Current Leaders: Sophia Segesta, Abby Adebawo

Q&A:

Q: Are artists of all skill levels welcome?

A: Yes!

Q: What mediums are club members allowed to work with?

A: Any medium is acceptable! This club is for artists to get together and have fun, regardless of how our art is made.

Q: Do I have to be pursuing art academically to join?

A: Nope! You're free to join whether you're an art major or a marine biologist.

Q: Are meetings mandatory?

A: Nope! We always appreciate more people at our meetings, but if you just want to join our Discord server, that's fine too!

Q: What do you guys even do at meetings?

A: Have fun! Whether that means just working on a personal project, homework, or playing drawing-based games as a group like Gartic Phone. Meetings, for the most part, are just for hanging out.
